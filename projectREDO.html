ABOUT THE DATA

TThe SAT is a standardized test widely used for college admissions in the United States. In this data base we show SAT results of 2012, in New York City. This data includes the name of the school, How many test takers they had, the average math score, and average writing score. We filtered out this data by including a search bar for users to search each school.

The most recent school level results for New York City on the SAT. Results are available at the school level for the graduating seniors of 2012. Records contain 2012 College-bound seniors average SAT scores taken during 2012.
